# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance, and it's essential to address any damage promptly to ensure the well-being of drivers and passengers. In this blog post, we'll discuss the importance of windshield safety, the benefits of chip repair, and when replacement is necessary.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A damaged windshield can obstruct the driver's view, increasing the risk of accidents
* Windshield damage can also compromise the structural integrity of the vehicle, making it more vulnerable to roof collapse in the event of a rollover
* In addition, a cracked or chipped windshield can lead to further damage from environmental factors such as temperature fluctuations and road debris
## Benefits of Chip Repair
* Chip repair is a cost-effective solution for small windshield damage, typically costing between $50-$100
* The process involves injecting a specialized resin into the damaged area, which seals the crack and prevents further spreading
* Chip repair can be completed quickly, usually within 30 minutes, and does not require replacing the entire windshield
## When to Replace Your Windshield
* If the damage is extensive, or the windshield is compromised, replacement is the safest option
* A cracked or chipped windshield that is larger than a dollar bill, or is located in the driver's line of sight, requires immediate attention and replacement
