# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance, as it plays a significant role in protecting drivers and passengers from injuries and accidents. At DFW Glass Mart, we understand the importance of proper auto glass care and want to provide you with helpful tips on how to prevent and repair windshield damage.
## Common Causes of Windshield Damage
There are several common causes of windshield damage, including:
* Road debris, such as rocks and pebbles
* Extreme weather conditions, like hail or intense sunlight
* Accidents and collisions
* Poor maintenance, such as neglecting to repair small chips or cracks
## Preventing Windshield Damage
While some types of windshield damage are unavoidable, there are steps you can take to minimize the risk of chips, cracks, and other forms of damage. Some tips include:
* Installing a windshield protector or cover
* Avoiding extreme temperatures and weather conditions
* Keeping a safe distance from other vehicles on the road
* Regularly inspecting your windshield for signs of damage
