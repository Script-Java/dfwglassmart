# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance. A damaged windshield can pose a significant risk to drivers and passengers. In this article, we will explore the importance of windshield safety, prevention methods, and repair options.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A cracked or broken windshield can obstruct the driver's view, leading to accidents.
* In the event of a crash, a weakened windshield can shatter and cause injuries.
* Windshield damage can also lead to airbag malfunction, further compromising safety.
## Prevention Methods
To prevent windshield damage, follow these tips:
* Avoid parking in extreme temperatures.
* Keep a safe distance from trucks carrying loose debris.
* Use a windshield sunshade to block out direct sunlight.
