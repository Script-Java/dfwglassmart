# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A small chip or crack can quickly spread, obstructing the driver's view and potentially leading to accidents.

## Why Chip Repair is Crucial
- **Prevents Further Damage**: A chip can turn into a long crack if not addressed promptly, necessitating a full windshield replacement.
- **Maintains Vehicle Integrity**: The windshield is a structural component of the vehicle, contributing to its overall stability and safety in the event of a collision.
- **Enhances Visibility**: Clear visibility is key to safe driving. Any obstruction, including chips and cracks, can increase the risk of accidents.
