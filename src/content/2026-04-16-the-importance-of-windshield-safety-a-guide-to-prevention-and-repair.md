      # Introduction to Windshield Safety
      As a vital component of vehicle safety, windshields play a significant role in protecting drivers and passengers from external hazards. At DFW Glass Mart, we understand the importance of maintaining your windshield in top condition.

      ## Why Windshield Safety Matters
      A damaged windshield can pose serious risks, including:
      * Impaired vision due to cracks or chips
      * Increased likelihood of shattering upon impact
      * Compromised structural integrity of your vehicle

      ## Prevention is Key
      To minimize the risk of windshield damage:
      * Avoid extreme temperature fluctuations
      * Park in shaded or covered areas
      * Use a windshield sunshade
      * Maintain a safe distance from large vehicles or construction zones

      ## Repair vs. Replacement
