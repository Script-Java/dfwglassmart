# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, putting you and your passengers at risk of injury or even fatal accidents. At DFW Glass Mart, we understand the importance of windshield safety and are committed to providing top-notch auto glass repair and replacement services.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A windshield provides up to 30% of a vehicle's structural strength, making it a vital component in the event of a crash or rollover.
* A damaged windshield can shatter easily, causing injuries from sharp glass fragments and compromising the deployment of airbags.
* In the event of a rollover, a weakened windshield can collapse, allowing the roof to cave in and causing serious head and neck injuries.
## Prevention is Key
To ensure windshield safety, it's essential to identify and address any issues promptly. Here are some tips for preventing windshield damage:
* Avoid driving over potholes and speed bumps, as these can cause stress fractures and weaken the glass.
* Park your vehicle in a shaded area or use a sunshade to reduce the risk of thermal stress, which can cause the glass to expand and contract.
* Use a microfiber cloth and gentle cleaning products to clean your windshield, avoiding abrasive materials that can scratch the glass.
