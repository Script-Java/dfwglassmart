# Introduction to Windshield Chip Repair
Windshield chip repair is a crucial aspect of vehicle maintenance that many drivers often overlook. Even a small chip in the windshield can lead to significant problems if left unattended. At DFW Glass Mart, we understand the importance of addressing windshield damage promptly to ensure safety on the road.
## Why Windshield Chips Matter
- **Safety**: A chip can obstruct the driver's view, especially if it is located in a critical area of the windshield.
- **Structural Integrity**: Chips can spread and compromise the windshield's structural integrity, potentially leading to a collapse.
- **Cost**: Repairing a chip is significantly less expensive than replacing the entire windshield.
## How Chip Repair Works
Chip repair involves cleaning the damaged area and injecting a special resin that bonds the glass together, preventing the chip from spreading. This process is not only cost-effective but also environmentally friendly, as it reduces waste by salvaging the existing windshield.
