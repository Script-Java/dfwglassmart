      # Introduction to Windshield Safety
      Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle and put you and your passengers at risk. In this article, we'll explore the importance of windshield safety and why timely repairs are essential.

      ## Why Windshield Safety Matters
      *   **Structural Integrity**: A windshield plays a significant role in maintaining the structural integrity of your vehicle. It provides support to the roof and helps to prevent it from collapsing in the event of a rollover.
      *   **Injury Prevention**: A damaged windshield can shatter and cause serious injuries to drivers and passengers. Even minor cracks can obstruct your view and increase the risk of accidents.
      *   **Cost Savings**: Repairing a windshield promptly can save you money in the long run. If left unrepaired, a small chip or crack can spread and require a full replacement, which is more expensive.

      ## Signs That Your Windshield Needs Repair
      *   **Chips and Cracks**: If you notice any chips or cracks in your windshield, it's essential to have them repaired as soon as possible.
      *   **Obstructed View**: If a chip or crack is obstructing your view, it's a sign that your windshield needs attention.
      *   **Leaks**: If you notice any leaks or water spots on your windshield, it may be a sign of a more significant issue.

      ## Conclusion
